Company History

We started out as "RifleCorp", a US Government subsidized company manufacturing assault rifles, bazookas, machine guns and other infantry equipment for the US Government during the Vietnam War in 1960s. We kept starting new production lines of weapons over time. The company started the Tanks and Armored Vehicles production line in 1969. Our first Jets and Helicopters went in to production in 1972. We also started producing civillian arms later on in the 1990s.

After realizing the horror's of Vietnam War and the effect it had on the patriotic and devoted members of the US Army, we renamed our company to "ShellShock Arms Corporation" in 1982 to raise awareness about the retired and ex military personnel that suffer from Post Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD). ShellShock is a term for psychological disturbance caused by prolonged exposure to warfare.
